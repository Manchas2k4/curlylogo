Introducing Curly Logo

Does your Logo run on Macs, Linux, and even PCs with Windows?

Does your Logo produce beautiful anti-aliased artwork?

Does your Logo let you change the width of the pen?

Is your Logo free?

In all the approved senses of the word?

Does your Logo require Absolutely No Installation?

Can you use your Logo from a public terminal?

Does your Logo let you use the elementary division, ÷, and multiplication, ×, signs in arithmetic expressions (like this: "100 × π ÷ n")?

Curly Logo does and is all of these things. Why aren't you using it?
